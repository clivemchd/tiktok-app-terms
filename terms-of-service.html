2. Description of Service

Shorts Pipeline is an automated content pipeline that:

  • Extracts pages from comic book files (PDF, CBZ, CBR)
  • Generates “Did You Know” narration scripts using AI vision analysis
  • Produces short-form videos with AI-generated voice narration
  • Publishes videos to TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram via their official APIs

The App operates as a command-line tool and uses platform APIs to publish content.
