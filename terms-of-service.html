2 Description of Service

Shorts Pipeline is an automated content pipeline that we developed to:

  • Extract pages from comic book files (PDF, CBZ, CBR)
  • Generate “Did You Know” narration scripts using AI vision analysis
  • Produce short-form videos with AI-generated voice narration
  • Publish videos to TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram via their official APIs

The App operates as a command-line tool and uses platform APIs to publish content on our behalf.
