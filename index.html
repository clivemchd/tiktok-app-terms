TikTok Shorts Pipeline

Welcome to the TikTok Shorts Pipeline application.

About the App

The TikTok Shorts Pipeline is a personal-use application that generates and posts short-form video content directly to your TikTok account via the TikTok API.

How It Works

  • You authenticate with your TikTok account using OAuth.
  • The app uses your OAuth token to post video content on your behalf.
  • You retain full ownership of all content created and posted through the app.

Your Data

We only collect TikTok OAuth tokens for authentication and posting purposes. We do not collect, store, or share any other personal data. You can revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings.
