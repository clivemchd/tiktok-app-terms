Welcome back, moneounchpan

The TikTok Shorts Pipeline is a personal-use application that generates and posts short-form video content directly to your TikTok account via the TikTok API.

How It Works

  • Authenticate with your TikTok account using OAuth.
  • The app posts video content on your behalf.
  • You retain full ownership of all content.

Your Data

We only collect TikTok OAuth tokens for authentication and posting. We do not collect, store, or share any other personal data. Revoke access any time via your TikTok settings.
