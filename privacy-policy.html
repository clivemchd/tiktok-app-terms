Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the TikTok Shorts Pipeline application ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

  • TikTok OAuth Tokens: We collect TikTok OAuth tokens solely for the purpose of authenticating and posting content to your TikTok account on your behalf.

We do not collect, store, or process any other personal data such as names, email addresses, browsing history, or device identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

The OAuth tokens we collect are used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account
  • Post video content to your TikTok account as directed by you

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data or OAuth tokens with any third parties.

Data Storage and Security

OAuth tokens are handled securely and are only retained for the duration necessary to perform the requested posting actions. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Your Rights

You may revoke the App's access to your TikTok account at any time through your TikTok account settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in this repository.
